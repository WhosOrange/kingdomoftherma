Getting To Know The Kingdom

Our Kingdom's history begins many centuries ago, well near the invention of stone tools. We've now since gained massive leaps in technology such as the burgeoning power of potential electric energy storage. The rite of first born sons carrying the "Therma" name has been practiced since this kingdom's establishment. King Therma XVI is our currrent ruler. In face his son, Therma XVII, personnally commissioned this very page.

The Realm of Therma

Within the realm of Therma lies mostly woodland. The Therma Castle and its surrounding township were constructed within the Therma Woods that make up a bulk of the realm. The long Therma River, sourced from the Therma Woods, snakes its way throughout. The south of the township borders its very shores. The Chocolate Falls east of the river leads towards the protected volcanic fields. The river eventually courses through Orph Territory, providing water to out neighbor.
