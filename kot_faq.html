Prince Therma XVII has recently gained an interest in technology. In addition, he's begun investigating many of the local rumors, such as the so called "metamorphics" or "fields of glass".

He is also known as a patron to many of the shops and eateries within the township. Although, most of his subject are not likely to know.

What is Orph Territory?

Orph is a neighboring state southeast of our Kingdom. They are on the bank of the Therma River. They are largely a city with minimal land within its borders. Their neutrality is famous throughout the world, fueled by their markets and culture of commerce. Orph has no monarchal system, with just an directly elected city council and a mayoral figure head.

What is Maze?

The Maze Organization was founded by Prince Therma as an independant offshoot of the Therma Kingdom defense services. Their goals are largely for surveying, archiving and expanding defense opportunities. Their establishment is largely considered a gift by the kingdom to other states (such as Oprh) who may not be able to provide their own. The Maze Organization Sovereign State (also known as MOS) was partitioned by Therma XVII to allow itself more autonomy and responsibility in actions.
